ABOUT THE BARKSHACK

The Barkshack is a full service pet care company!

Karen Just is the owner of The Bark Shack in Bluffton, South Carolina, offering dog and cat grooming services in Beaufort County. Karen Just, is a certified pet groomer, a member of the National Dog Groomers Association, and a member of National Association of Creative Pet Groomers. Karen dreamed of owning a kennel since the age of 9, while living in Chattanooga, TN, but life, 8 years as a radio announcer, and 20 years in corporate sales got in the way. Finally, starting in 2007, Karen created/owned and sold a profitable kennel, then embarked on her latest and best "The Bark Shack and The Bark Shack Outdoors." Karen has two gorgeous girls and one totally adorable grandson!...and three rescue dogs….
